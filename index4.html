How we test

Compliance checked
at every stage

Standards aren't applied once at the end — they're built into the same four stages every job runs through.

01

Spec review

Your artwork and dimensions are checked against the relevant standard before proofing begins.

02

Material verification

Substrate batches are matched to their rated durability and adhesion certificates.

03

In-run sampling

Samples are pulled at intervals through the print run to check registration and colour accuracy.

04

Final sign-off

A quality lead signs off the batch against the standard before it's cleared for dispatch.
