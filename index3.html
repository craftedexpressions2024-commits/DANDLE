Our story

Started by people who were tired of reprints

Dandle Printing started with a simple frustration: too many print jobs came back slightly wrong — a label that didn't quite fit, a colour that didn't match, a material that failed outdoors within a year. We built our process around catching those problems before they leave the building.

Today that means every job runs through the same proofing, calibration and quality checks, whether it's a single custom nameplate or a production run of ten thousand labels. We don't treat consistency as a selling point — we treat it as the minimum standard.

We're still run by people who pick up the phone, still proof every job before it prints, and still stand behind every label that leaves our floor.

How we work

Professional by
process, not by promise

The same four checks run on every job, regardless of size or client.

01

Every job is proofed

No print run starts until dimensions, colour and material are confirmed against your spec in writing.

02

Every press is calibrated

Equipment is checked on a fixed schedule, not just when something looks off.

03

Every batch is inspected

Samples are pulled through the run, not just checked once at the end.

04

Every client has one point of contact

You deal with the same person from quote to dispatch, so nothing gets lost between departments.
